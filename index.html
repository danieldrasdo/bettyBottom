For over 50 years, Betty Bottom Showroom has served specialty retailers in North America by providing modern, updated women's apparel.

About Us!

1965

Once upon a time...

In 1965, Betty Bottom Showroom was established by a very vivacious lady named Betty Bottom. This was at a time when multiple line showrooms were not very common, she was definitely a pioneer.

The showroom is sold

After several successful years, Betty Bottom sold her showroom to her employee Berda Morley, who partnered with Rosanne Tritica. With the sale of Betty Bottom Showroom came a decade of very successful years, such as Betty Bottom Showroom's first milestone of representing over 20 different clothing lines.

Betty Bottom is acquired

In 1990 Rosanne Tritica and Ernesto Mantilla, who at the time was a customer of Betty Bottom Showroom, partnered and acquired the showroom from Berda Morley.

Our showroom gets a new look

At the end of 2015, and with the new concept of California Market Center consolidating fashion showrooms in the 3A section of the property, Betty Bottom Showroom begins a new and exciting venture by signing a 10 year lease on a beautiful showroom facing Los Angeles Street with a more contemporary and open feeling, all while maintaining its original art-inspired, friendly attitude.

Construction is set to be completed at the beginning of 2016

today
